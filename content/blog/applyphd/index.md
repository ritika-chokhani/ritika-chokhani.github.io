## Overview

I sometimes get questions about how to search for and apply for doctoral (PhD) programs. I thought I would create a short post describing my experience of searching for and applying for programs in the UK in 2022-2023. 

I chose to apply to the UK because I had already done my Masters here and hence felt a sense of familiarity both with living here and being in the academic system here. So I decided to apply to the UK. The following description is based on my experience in the UK. 

## What is a PhD?

There are many who have answered this question better than me, but in short, a PhD is an original contribution to knowledge. I like pointing out this aspect of a PhD because I think it's also what makes it so exciting - that you can discover something new and be on the cutting edge of knowledge in your field. 

In the UK, a clinical doctorate (often called a PsyD) is different from a research PhD (often called a PhD).
